# Mergington High School Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment for
our school community, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to
make participation in the Extra-Curricular Activities project a
respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on students' benefit
- Showing empathy

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Inappropriate language
- Personal attacks
- Harassment
- Sharing private info
- Any harmful behavior

## Responsibilities

Maintainers must enforce rules fairly and remove harmful content.

## Scope

Applies in all project spaces and when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report issues to IT Club advisor. All cases are reviewed fairly.
